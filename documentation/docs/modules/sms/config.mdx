# Configuration


All you need is .., not love,but two configuration account credentials. <br />
- Account SID
- Auth Token

You can find them in your twilio console.<br />
Do not forget to fill your phone number that corresponds to your twilio account.

## Setting up Twilio Provider

Just fill the fields with your account credentials and click save!<br />

![Twilio config](/img/sms/twilio_config.png)

You are now ready to send sms.
