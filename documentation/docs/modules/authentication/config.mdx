# Config
## Auth Config

- **Token Invalidation Period:** You can change the live duration of the token.

![Enable sign-in method](/img/authentication/auth_config.png)
## Enable sign in method
You can configure  the authentication module through the admin panel.<br />
Depending on the sign-in method desired, toggle the corresponding button.<br />
You may optionally enable email verification.

![Enable sign-in method](/img/authentication/enable_sign_in_method.png)

## How to enable 2FA?
Find the user you are interested in, press the edit button on the right side of the panel and<br />
a pop-up window will be displayed to you. In order to enable it, you should fill the phone number<br />
section  with your actual phone number. <br />

![Enable sign-in methods](/img/authentication/enable2FA.png)

Users may now  individually [enable](./api_reference/user/local/#enable-2fa)  2FA
for their accounts.

## Third party

You may third party authentication options through the admin panel by enabling the corresponding provider.<br />
You may also enable account linking.

![Third party Configuration](/img/authentication/facebook_enable.png)
