# Overview

## What module Email is?
<p> Many web applications need a way to send automatically e-mails to users.
Email module serves this purpose. It is used for sending e-mails base on templates or not via any plugin or code.
</p>

## Supported Providers
Mailgun, Sendgrid, Mandrill and Smtp mail providers are supported.


## Features
- Sending emails
- Creating your own email templates.
- Uploading your email templates on email provider account.
- Downloading created email templates from email provider to your local database.
- Email variables are also supported.
